# Title
PAC-ier MAN

## Repository
https://github.com/T8Destroyer/ANGM_2305-Final_Project-T8Destroyer.git

## Description
This will be a simple recreation of the classic arcade game PAC-MAN, with new enemies to run from. This will add 4 new kinds of ghosts with unique original behaviors, and give the player the ability to choose what ghosts they will face.

## Features
- Feature 1
	- Play PAC-MAN. This will be the biggest step, involving understanding the original game's game loop, and the behavior of every ghost.
- Feature 2
	- Have 4 new ghosts. Once I recreate PAC-MAN, it will then be a matter of designing new AI behaviors for the 4 new ghosts based on the framework I made.

## Challenges
- Making PacMan move will require knowing how to get player input
- Recreating the maze and correctly placing pellets will require knowing how level creation works in python/pygame.
- Recreating the maze will also require knowing how collisions are handled in python/pygame. 
- Recreating the AI of the 4 original ghosts will require studying the original game, then translating that to python.
- Making my own ghost AI will require knowing how the original ghosts work
- Making a "choose your own ghosts" menu will involve knowing how in-game buttons and loading the actual game works
- Using in-game sprites and audio will involve knowing how to import and display/play them

## Outcomes
Ideal Outcome:
- Recreate PAC-MAN with 4 new ghosts, complete with a "choose you own ghosts" menu and sprites/sfx

Minimal Viable Outcome:
- Recreate a simple version of PAC-MAN with at least one of the original ghosts
